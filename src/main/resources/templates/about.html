About

Welcome to Art Gallery, your premier destination for art enthusiasts and administrators alike. Our platform is designed to provide a seamless experience for users to explore, purchase, and manage artworks, while offering robust tools for administrators to curate, update, and oversee the gallery's inventory.

For Users

Discover a curated selection of captivating artworks and easily add them to your cart for purchase. Our user-friendly interface ensures a hassle-free browsing and shopping experience. Register today to unlock exclusive features and stay updated on the latest additions to our collection.

For Admins

Take control of your art gallery with our comprehensive admin dashboard. Add new products, edit existing listings, and manage inventory effortlessly. Our intuitive tools empower you to create a dynamic and engaging platform for art enthusiasts, while ensuring seamless backend management.
